About us

GT Modellista offers a fun and inclusive throw back to the golden era of web based car forums. Before social media became king and 4G existed the eaiest way to connect with a large community of like minded car enthusiasts was through a forum. There are many forums still buzzing with activity but little off a blanket inclusivity. GT Modellista welcomes petrol heads from all walks of life! From your American Muscle, to your JDM icon and everything inbetwee, share your builds with us through a Build Thread.
